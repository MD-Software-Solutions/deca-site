Fall Rally

What is Fall Rally?

The DECA Fall Rally is an exciting event organized by Georgia DECA, aimed at preparing emerging leaders and entrepreneurs for careers in marketing, finance, hospitality, and management.

When and Where?

Fall Rally will take place on October 2, 2024 at Six Flags Over Georgia.
